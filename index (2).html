About Us

Welcome to Sol y Mar Hotel, a sustainable beachfront retreat nestled between the sea and the forest. Designed for those seeking peace, connection, and eco-conscious luxury, our boutique hotel offers a true escape from the digital world.

Powered by solar energy, built with reclaimed wood, and serving 100% organic meals, we are committed to preserving the natural beauty of our surroundings. From sunrise yoga on the sand to stargazing by the fire pit, every moment here is crafted to help you unplug and reconnect.
